---
name: specs-no-content
description: Specifications must describe technical behavior only — product names, flavor text, and domain branding (e.g. "Co-Author", "DM Screen") are content, not specification.
type: feedback
---

When writing specifications, do not include content-level details like product
names, character names, branding, or flavor text. Specs describe what the
system does technically, not what things are called in the UI or domain.
