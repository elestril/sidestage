The Cracked Tankard

Lamplight pools on scarred oak tables and the air hums with low conversation. Behind the long bar, kegs of dark ale sweat in the warmth of the hearth. A perfect place to listen for rumors — or to be overheard sharing them.

  • Thrain Ironfoot
    *shoulders against the bar, eyeing the room* You sure about this place, sage?
  • Elara Moonwhisper
    The runes carved into the doorpost suggest the proprietor knows old wards. We're safer here than in the streets.
  • Thrain Ironfoot
    Wards. Hmph. I'll trust the weight of my axe over scratches in wood any day.
  • Elara Moonwhisper
    And yet you've kept that axe sharp because of a vision I shared with you in Velhern. Trust comes in many forms.
  • Thrain Ironfoot
    *long pause, then a reluctant grin* Aye. Order me an ale, then. Let's see what rumors the tankard floats up.
